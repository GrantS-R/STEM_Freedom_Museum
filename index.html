Welcome

Welcome to The STEM Education Museum. We aspire to shine a light on STEM education, in order to pave a brighter path for this wonderful new field.

Intro

STEM education is important because it helps prepare students for the workforce, especially if those jobs are in a STEM or STEM adjacent field. Freedom in STEM education is important because it ensures that all people have an equally fair chance at STEM education, and thus STEM related jobs or just the workforce in general.
