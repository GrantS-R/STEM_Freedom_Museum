James Webb Space Telescope

The James Webb Space Telescope, shown above, was in consideration even before the Hubble Space Telescope was launched in 1990. Webb lets us:

  • See deeper into space than ever before.
  • See "in to the past."
  • Get clearer photos of deep space than ever before.

Webb's studies of cost and fesability began in 1999, and the construction of the first pars of Webb began until 2004. After some issues and upgrades during development as well as testing on earth in vaccums, Webb finally launched on December 25, 2021.
